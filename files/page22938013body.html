Solanium Legacy
A collection of 3500 random generated playable NFT on the Solana blockchain.
Launching: TBA
What is Solanium Legacy ?
Solanium Legacy is p2e 2d roguelike, Action Role Playing Game with a dynamic combat style.
There will be 2 fractions: Union and Revenant. First mint will be with Union Adventurers, second with Revenant. 70% of royalties will be sent to the prize pool for seasonal reward. Players will have an oportunity to create guilds and compete for seasonal reward. Only top 3 guilds of 1 fraction will get seasonal reward (1st place - 60%, 2nd - 30%, 3rd - 10% of prize pool).
